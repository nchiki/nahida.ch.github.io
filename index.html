Experience

Work Experience

Summer Tech Analyst (Cloud Engineer) at Goldman Sachs June-September 2018

During the last summer, I worked in the SecDb Architecture team as a Cloud Engineer (Summer Tech Analyst). My project was about integrating Protobuf into Slang to support the integration of gRPC into Slang. Slang is Goldman's proprietary programming language. The purpose of my project was to enable teams to move towards a more service-based architecture. To do that, I implemented functions in C++ to dynamically parse .proto files and generate Slang code. I exposed those functions to Slang in order to be able to modify proto messages and serialize/deserialize them inside Slang scripts. It was especially difficult since Slang is dynamically-typed, the scripts live in databases and it all had to work in SecView, an editor for Slang scripts.

CodeU Participant at Google 2017-2018

Google's "CodeU" program is an exclusive, invitation-only 10-week technical development program for high potential students with mentorship from Google Engineers. I got the chance to collaborate in a team of three peers and a Google Engineer to solve multiple coding assignments that tested my knowledge of fundamental algorithms and data structures. These assignments can be found on my github. In addition to that, I was invited into the Google London Office to listen to presentations and engage with other Google Engineers. I also had the chance to participate in multiple mock interviews.

Brand Ambassador and Camp Tutor at Firetech 2017-2018

Firetech is a UK holiday camp and tech course provider with the mission of teaching children and teens about technology and computer science. I represent Firetech at recruitment and marketing events to liaise with potential recruits, customers and partners and represent Firetech and its mission to all stakeholders. I also worked as a tutor teaching multiple courses. I taught children aged between 9 and 13 about technology and game development using Java and the Greenfoot IDE. In addition to that, I taught Video Game Design using Construct 2, a game editor, and a Teen Java Club during term on the weekends.

Hack-a-Project Participant at Facebook 2017-2018

Facebook's "Hack-a-project" is a five week program taking place at the Facebook office in London.
